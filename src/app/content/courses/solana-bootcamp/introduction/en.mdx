# Introduction

Welcome to the 2026 Solana Technical Bootcamp!

The Solana Technical Bootcamp is co-hosted by **Solar** and the **Solana Foundation**, supported by **MagicBlock**, and brought together with 21 leading developer communities and 21 university blockchain associations across the Chinese-speaking region. This six-month, three-season development program is tailored for Chinese-speaking developers, progressing from beginner to advanced levels, helping Solana developers master end-to-end product design and project development, and preparing them for developer roles in the Solana ecosystem.

Courses are taught by experienced developers and ecosystem mentors from the Solana Foundation, with weekly Office Hours for live Q&A. 10+ top Chinese-speaking projects offer on-the-spot hiring upon graduation, and 500+ Solana Job Board projects provide referrals for graduates. Graduates who submit hackathon projects on behalf of the Chinese-speaking community receive NFT graduation certificates, Solana ecosystem project airdrops and priority access, as well as generous developer support scholarships.

- **Season 1**: Blockchain Technology 101 (Jan 6 – Feb 6)
- **Season 2**: Blockchain Project Practice (Mar 5 – Apr 5)
- **Season 3**: Solana Enterprise Technology Practice (May 5 – Jun 5)

### Solana

Solana is one of the world's top three public blockchains (alongside BTC and Ethereum), renowned for its high performance, low cost, and strong scalability. With its innovative technical architecture and active developer ecosystem, Solana has built a comprehensive on-chain ecosystem spanning decentralized finance (DeFi), decentralized physical infrastructure networks (DePIN), payments, and Web3 consumer applications.

Solana is committed to becoming the foundational infrastructure for the next generation of financial systems, driving the formation of Internet Capital Markets. Across key metrics such as on-chain stablecoin circulation, asset minting, developer growth, ecosystem application revenue, new asset issuance, transaction volume, and active user counts, Solana consistently ranks among the industry leaders, demonstrating outstanding ecosystem vitality and innovation potential.

MagicBlock is a next-generation infrastructure layer built for Solana, enabling real-time, Web2-like applications with support for both private and public execution. Powered by ephemeral rollups, MagicBlock delivers ultra-low latency, near-zero gas fees, and high throughput while preserving Solana's security, composability, and shared liquidity. This unlocks a new category of Web3 applications—such as fully on-chain games, real-time DeFi, scalable DePIN, and consumer apps—that feel instant and seamless.
